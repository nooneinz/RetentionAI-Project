Student Retention & Early Intervention System

RetentionAI's next-generation predictive analytical gateway. Powered by a hybrid Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) sequential deep learning architecture, we process high-dimensional student academic indicators in real-time. Securely integrated into a live transactional database, this portal mitigates student attrition, maps retention curves, and activates early pedagogical support channels.

Institutional Ecosystem & Role Matrix

The Flask-based system delivers secure, role-based access control (RBAC) to support critical academic monitoring and pedagogical intervention procedures.

System Administrator

Responsible for secure user provisioning, initial cohort course configuration, and database integrity. The administrator uploads verified coursework CSV files, triggers model re-training pipelines, and audits system-wide transactional logging.

Academic Lecturer

Instructors command the core teaching dashboard. They log physical lecture attendance, record module marks, verify student evaluations, and directly dispatch warning notices or private interactive counseling chat triggers.

Student Profile

Provides students with a transparent view of their own academic progression. Through their portal, students review dynamic risk indicators, respond to counselor alerts, join collaboration groups, and complete usability evaluations.

System Scope & Description

This analytical student retention gateway evaluates continuous indicators across coursework assignments and physical lecture attendance. It maps student risk profiles to support structured teaching interventions and secure administrative review.

All predictive models are evaluated inside isolated, transactional SQLite/MySQL schemas to satisfy academic research ethics and administrative compliance.
